What is CROW?

CROW is an interdisciplinary research collective at Presidency University, Kolkata, where passionate students explore the frontiers of computational science.

We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, fostering innovation through collaborative research, hands-on projects, and cutting-edge exploration.

Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of computational researchers and problem solvers.
